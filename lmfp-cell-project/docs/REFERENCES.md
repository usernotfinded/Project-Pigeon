# References

## Cathode — LMFP doping and coating

1. **Mg doping**: Gonçalves et al., "Emerging multimetal LMFP-based cathodes for lithium-ion batteries: a review," *J. Mater. Chem. A* 13, 40399–40429 (2025). DOI: 10.1039/D5TA03194J
   - MgNi-LMFP/C: 92% retention after 2,000 cycles at 1C in full cell

2. **Nb doping**: Liang et al., "Boosting High-Temperature Durability of Industrial-Scale LiMn₀.₆Fe₀.₄PO₄ Cathode through Niobium Doping," *ACS Appl. Mater. Interfaces* 17(23), 33783–33795 (2025). DOI: 10.1021/acsami.4c22977
   - 2% Nb: 95.07% retention over 150 cycles at 60°C, industrial-scale coprecipitation

3. **Ni/Co co-doping**: Chen et al., "Enhanced cycling stability and Li⁺ diffusion kinetics in LiMn₀.₆Fe₀.₄PO₄ cathode via co-doping strategy of Ni²⁺ and Co²⁺," *Chem. Eng. J.* (2025). DOI: 10.1016/S1385-8947(25)10843-7
   - LMFP-NiCo: 85.4% retention after 700 cycles at 2C

4. **Ti-doping + LiTiOPO₄ coating**: Wei et al., "Inhibiting manganese dissolution in LiFe₀.₄Mn₀.₆PO₄ through synergistic effect of Ti-doping and LiTiOPO₄-coating," *Chem. Eng. J.* (2025). DOI: 10.1016/S2405-8297(25)00689-0
   - 92.4% after 900 cycles at 1C; 99.6% after 300 cycles at 45°C

5. **LFP surface coating**: OAEPublish review, "LiMn₁₋ᵧFeᵧPO₄ rechargeable batteries: bridging material innovation with practical cell design," *Energy Mater.* (2025). DOI: 10.20517/energymater.2025.29
   - LFP coating reduced Mn/Fe dissolution by >50% in full-cell tests at 60°C

## Electrolyte — LiFSI and Mn dissolution

6. **LiFSI/LiPF₆ 95/5 blend**: Leslie et al., "Reducing the Rate of Mn Dissolution in LiMn₀.₈Fe₀.₂PO₄/Graphite Cells with Mixed Salt and Low Salt Molarity Electrolytes," *J. Electrochem. Soc.* 172, 040515 (2025). DOI: 10.1149/1945-7111/adc951
   - 5% LiPF₆ + 95% LiFSI showed best combination of Al passivation and Mn dissolution reduction

7. **LiDFOB in LMFP systems**: OAEPublish review (ref. 5 above)
   - ADN-based electrolytes with LiDFOB enabled stable cycling of LMFP/graphite full cells

8. **LiBOB as HF scavenger**: OSTI report, "LiBOB in high capacity LIBs" (2024). DOI: 10.2172/2429450

## Anode — Graphite/hard carbon hybrid

9. **Graphite/HC hybrid**: Chen et al., "Enabling 6C Fast Charging of Li-Ion Batteries with Graphite/Hard Carbon Hybrid Anodes," *Adv. Energy Mater.* 11(5), 2003336 (2021). DOI: 10.1002/aenm.202003336

10. **Spherical HC/graphite**: PLOS ONE, "Spherical hard carbon/graphite anode for high performance lithium ion batteries" (2024). DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0311943

11. **SHS capsule additive**: CCS Chemistry, "Seeded and Confined Li Plating Enabled by Capsule Additives: Achieving Safe 6C Charging on 180 μm-Thick Graphite Anodes" (2025). DOI: 10.31635/ccschem.025.202506212
